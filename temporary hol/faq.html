Frequently Asked Questions

There are many questions which come up within the community time and time again, as well as situations that we all find ourselves in where we might look to the Synagogue for help and guidance. We hope this page offers the answers and advice you need, however if you can't find what you are looking for feel free to email Carolyn in the shul office.

Can I join the burial society without becoming a member of the Shul? See answer

There is no way of joining the burial society without becoming a member of the shul. But why don't you come along to one of our services as you might really enjoy being a member at Watford!

How long does it takes for a membership application to be processed? See answer

This varies. It can take two weeks, but sometimes the United Synagogue need proof of jewish status, i.e. parents' marriage certificate, applicant's birth certificate, and if that is the case it can take longer.

Hide this content.

When do you collect membership fees? See answer

We bill our members annually at Pesach. Anyone who joins during the year pays pro rata.

Hide this content.

Do I have to pay my membership fees in one lump sum? See answer

We bill our members annually at Pesach and this can be paid by monthly direct debit, cheque (some post dated if wanted) or credit card. Anyone who joins during the year pays pro rata.
