Wads Cares
We are very anxious that WADS Community keeps in touch with the news. We can visit people who would like to have a visitor; we can help in many ways. What we really need is for you to let us know of the joys and sorrows that happen within our Synagogue, without this contact we cannot possibly know what is happening.
