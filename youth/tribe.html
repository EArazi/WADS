TRIBE

Tribe has four divisions, each with its own events and activities, designed to be as exciting as possible!

  • Tribe Babies & Tots: From first nappy to last day at primary school
  • Tribe School Years: School from start to finish
  • Tribe Campus: Freshers fairs through to graduation glory

    • We believe that young Jews should:

    • be proud of being Jewish
    • enjoy vibrant orthodoxy in contemporary British society
    • belong to a community of their peers
    • feel welcome irrespective of their observance
    • be educated and inspired about their Judaism and Israel
    • feel that their local shul is warm and inviting
    • contribute to the welfare of their community
    • have Jewish grandchildren
    • value their membership of Tribe and the United Synagogue


    Are you up for joining Tribe?

    Membership of Tribe is free and brings with it a package of benefits to enhance not only your daily life but also your Jewish life. Tribe Community Membership is £5 a month and includes additional benefits and full membership to a Untied Synagogue Shul of your choice.
